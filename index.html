About Our Program

After 20 years of attempts by the Hillsborough community, Aaron Haldar successfully founded the HHS Odyssey of the Mind program in 2024 along with co-founders Aarav and Arnav Chamunya. In our first year, we made history by:

  • Competing at Regionals and States as a first-year team
  • Building an engineering solution that held 310 pounds
  • Growing from 12 to 30 members in our second year
  • Establishing partnerships with local schools and businesses

Our Competitions

Problem 4: The Structure Challenge

Our team built a balsa wood structure that held 310 pounds at States, placing 2nd in Regionals. Aaron Haldar designed the engineering solution while Pranet Godavarty and Arjun Nandurkar led construction.

Problem 5: Stellar Stories

Created an original performance combining Greek and Hindu astronomy myths, featuring handmade props by Owen Sparks and Hania Bilal.
